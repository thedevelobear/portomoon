🧭 Read first — what changed for 2026

  • Madeira hikes need paid timed reservations. Every classified PR trail is booked in advance on the official SIMplifica portal (€4.50pp, €10.50 for PR1). Save the ticket offline — no signal in the mountains.
  • Benagil Cave is licensed-tour-only. Swimming and self-guided entry have been banned and fined since 2024. Only small boats can get inside.
  • Pena Palace is strict timed entry. Interior slots sell out days ahead in summer; the Lisboa Card doesn't cover it.
